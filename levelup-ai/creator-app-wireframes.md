# Creator Command Center - Wireframes & Design

## APP OVERVIEW
**Name:** Creator Command Center  
**Tagline:** Schedule smarter. Create faster.  
**Target:** Content creators, solopreneurs, small influencers  
**Price:** $10-15/month (or freemium: free for 1 platform, $12 for unlimited)

## KEY DESIGN PRINCIPLES

1. **Simple** — One screen = one job
2. **Fast** — Schedule a post in < 30 seconds
3. **Visual** — Calendar view, not list view (like Later)
4. **AI-Assisted** — Suggestions, not replacements
5. **Creator-Focused** — No agency/business bloat
