## 💡 Quick Win: Turn Meeting Notes into Action Items (5 Minutes)

**The Problem:** You take notes in meetings, then never look at them again. Action items get lost.

**The Fix:** Use AI to extract tasks automatically.

**3-Step Implementation:**

1. **Copy your meeting notes** (from Zoom, Google Meet, or handwritten)

2. **Paste into ChatGPT/Claude with this prompt:**
   ```
   Review these meeting notes and extract:
   - Action items with owner names
   - Deadlines mentioned
   - Decisions made
   - Questions that need follow-up
   
   Format as a checklist I can copy into my task manager.
   ```

3. **Copy the output** into your task manager (Todoist, Asana, Trello, or even a notes app)

**Time saved:** 10-15 minutes per meeting  
**Bonus:** Nothing falls through the cracks anymore
