## 💡 Quick Win: The "Act As" Prompt Trick

**The problem:** ChatGPT gives generic, boring responses

**The fix:** Tell it WHO to be before you ask the question

**Instead of:** "Write an email to a customer about a delayed order"

**Try:** "Act as a customer service manager who deeply cares about customer satisfaction. Write a sincere email to a customer about their delayed order, offering a small discount for the inconvenience."

**The result:** Specific, on-brand, human-sounding content. Every time.

**Your turn:** Try this with your next ChatGPT prompt. Just add "Act as a [role] who [specific traits]" at the start.
