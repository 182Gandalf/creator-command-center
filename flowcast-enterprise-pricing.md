## Enterprise Plan Pricing Guidelines

### Base Enterprise Tier
- **Price Range:** €499 - €999 / month (annual billing)
- **Target:** Teams of 10-50 users
- **Includes:**
  - Unlimited users
  - Unlimited scheduled posts
  - Unlimited AI content ideas
  - Priority support (24-hour response)
  - Custom integrations (API access)
  - Dedicated account manager
  - SLA: 99.9% uptime guarantee
  - Advanced analytics & reporting

### Enterprise Plus Tier
- **Price Range:** €1,499 - €2,999 / month (annual billing)
- **Target:** Teams of 50-200 users
- **Includes everything in Enterprise, plus:**
  - White-label options
  - Custom AI model training
  - Advanced security features (SSO, audit logs)
  - SLA: 99.95% uptime guarantee
  - Priority support (4-hour response)
  - Quarterly business reviews
  - Custom onboarding & training

### Enterprise Elite Tier
- **Price Range:** €5,000+ / month (annual billing)
- **Target:** Large organizations 200+ users
- **Includes everything in Enterprise Plus, plus:**
  - Unlimited API calls
  - Custom feature development
  - Dedicated infrastructure option
  - SLA: 99.99% uptime guarantee
  - 24/7 phone support
  - On-site training available
  - Executive business reviews
