## 1. WHAT IS CAC?

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** = Total Sales & Marketing Spend / Number of New Customers Acquired

For FlowCast, this includes all costs to get a user to sign up AND convert to a paid plan.

### Channel 2: Paid Social Ads (Meta, TikTok)

| Cost Item | Estimated |
|-----------|-----------|
| Cost Per Click (CPC) | €0.50-1.50 |
| Landing Page CTR | 2-5% |
| Sign-up Conversion | 10-20% |
| Paid Conversion Rate | 5-10% |
| **Projected CAC** | **€50-150** |

**Calculation:**
```
€1 CPC → 100 clicks = €100
100 clicks × 3% CTR = 3 signups
3 signups × 15% paid = 0.45 customers
CAC = €100 / 0.45 = €222
```

**Optimized (good targeting + landing page):**
```
€0.80 CPC → 100 clicks = €80
100 clicks × 5% CTR = 5 signups
5 signups × 10% paid = 0.5 customers
CAC = €80 / 0.5 = €160
```

**Pros:** Immediate results, scalable  
**Cons:** Expensive, stops when you stop paying  
**Best for:** Testing messaging, quick wins

### Channel 4: Referral Program (Viral Loop)

| Item | Value |

**Pros:** Lowest CAC, self-sustaining  
**Cons:** Takes time to build momentum

## 6. LIFETIME VALUE (LTV) CALCULATION

### Formula
**LTV = (Monthly Revenue × Gross Margin) / Monthly Churn Rate**

### By Plan

| Plan | Monthly | Gross Margin | Churn (est.) | LTV | CAC Target | LTV/CAC |
|------|---------|--------------|--------------|-----|------------|---------|
| Starter | $6 | 64% | 8% | $48 | €15 | 3.2:1 |
| Creator | $15 | 63% | 5% | $189 | €40 | 4.7:1 |
| Pro | $29 | 60% | 3% | $580 | €80 | 7.2:1 |

**Analysis:**
- **Starter:** Tight margins, need to keep CAC under €15
- **Creator:** Healthy unit economics, CAC up to €40 works
- **Pro:** Excellent margins, can spend more to acquire

## 8. CAC OPTIMIZATION STRATEGIES

### Quick Wins (Implement Immediately)

1. **Referral Program**
   - 1 free month for referrer + 1 free month for new user
   - Expected: 20% referral rate
   - CAC: €6-15 (vs €30-50 paid ads)

2. **Annual Discount**
   - 2 months free for annual billing
   - Locks in customer for 12 months
   - Reduces effective CAC by 17%

3. **Product-Led Growth**
   - Free trial with 20 posts
   - Show value before asking for money
   - Increases conversion from 5% to 10-15%
   - **Cuts CAC in half**

### Medium-Term (Months 3-6)

4. **Content Marketing**
   - YouTube tutorials
   - Blog posts about creator economy
   - SEO for "content scheduler"
   - Target CAC: €10-15 (vs €30-50 ads)

5. **Creator Partnerships**
   - Affiliate program: 30% commission
   - Only pay when they convert
   - CAC: €15-20 (but no upfront cost)

6. **Onboarding Optimization**
   - Reduce time-to-value
   - Increase activation rate
   - Better onboarding = higher conversion = lower CAC

### Long-Term (Months 6-12)

7. **Word-of-Mouth**
   - Build a great product
   - Delight customers
   - Organic referrals drive CAC toward €0

8. **Channel Optimization**
   - Double down on lowest CAC channels
   - Cut expensive channels
   - Target blended CAC: €15-20

## 12. RED FLAGS & ADJUSTMENTS

### If CAC is Too High (>€50)
- Cut underperforming ad campaigns
- Double down on organic/referrals
- Improve landing page conversion
- Test cheaper channels

### If CAC is Increasing
- Market saturation (expand channels)
- Competition heating up
- Ad fatigue (refresh creative)
- Seasonal effects

### If Payback Period > 12 Months
- Reduce CAC
- Increase prices
- Improve retention (reduce churn)
- Move customers to annual plans
