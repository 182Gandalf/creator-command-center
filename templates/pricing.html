Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, all paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers (EU only). All payments processed securely via Paddle.

Can I get a refund?

We offer refunds within 14 days of your first payment if you're not satisfied. See our Refund Policy for details.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes! Pay annually and get 2 months free (17% discount). Creator plan drops from €108 to just €90/year. Contact us for annual billing options.

How do you compare to Buffer?

We're 40% cheaper! Buffer charges $15/month (€14) for 3 channels with limited posts. Our Creator plan is €9/month with unlimited posts and includes AI content generation.

What's the difference between GPT-3.5 and GPT-4?

GPT-3.5 (Starter & Creator) is fast and great for most content ideas. GPT-4 (Pro) is more creative and better for complex content strategies. Both generate high-quality suggestions!
