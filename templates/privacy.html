1. Who We Are

FlowCast ("we," "us," or "our") is a social media scheduling platform operated by FlowCast. We're committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Account creation date
  • Subscription tier and billing information

2.2 Content You Create

  • Post drafts and scheduled content
  • Media uploads (images, videos)
  • Content ideas and AI-generated suggestions you save
  • Posting schedules and preferences

2.3 Social Media Tokens (With Your Consent)

When you connect social media accounts, we store OAuth tokens to publish on your behalf. These tokens are:

  • Encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Only used to perform actions you explicitly request
  • Automatically revoked when you disconnect an account

2.4 Usage Data

  • IP address (for security and abuse prevention)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and features used
  • API request logs (anonymized after 30 days)

4. Social Media Integrations

🔐 You Control Your Connections

You can connect or disconnect social media accounts at any time from your dashboard. When you disconnect:

  • We immediately delete your OAuth tokens from our servers
  • Your scheduled posts for that platform are paused
  • Your content drafts remain available (you own them)

4.1 YouTube Integration

When you connect YouTube, we request permission to:

  • Upload videos to your channel
  • View your channel information
  • Manage your YouTube content (only actions you explicitly request)

We comply with the YouTube API Services Terms of Service.

4.2 Instagram Integration

When you connect Instagram, we request permission to:

  • Publish posts and stories to your account
  • View basic account information

We only support Instagram Business/Creator accounts via the Instagram Graph API.

4.3 Other Platforms

We integrate with other platforms following the same principles: minimal permissions, encrypted storage, and your explicit consent for every action.

5. Data Storage & Security

5.1 Encryption

  • Data in transit: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers uses TLS 1.3 encryption
  • Data at rest: Your OAuth tokens and sensitive data are encrypted using AES-256
  • Passwords: Hashed using bcrypt with salt

5.2 Infrastructure

  • Servers located in EU (GDPR compliant)
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access logging and monitoring
  • Automated backups with encryption

5.3 Data Breach Procedures

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Report to relevant authorities as required by law
  • Take immediate steps to secure the breach
  • Provide transparent updates on our response

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you have certain rights regarding your data:

6.1 GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)

  • Right to access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: Delete your account and data
  • Right to restrict processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability: Export your data
  • Right to object: Opt out of certain processing

6.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Know if your information is sold or shared (we don't)
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

6.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

You can exercise most rights directly in your account settings. For other requests, email us at privacy@flowcast.space. We respond to all requests within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

FlowCast is not intended for children under 13 (or 16 in the EU). We don't knowingly collect data from children. If you believe we've collected data from a child, contact us immediately and we'll delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy
