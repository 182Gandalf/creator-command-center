Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

1. Our Commitment

We want you to be completely satisfied with FlowCast. This Refund Policy outlines when and how you can request a refund.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee: If you are not satisfied with FlowCast for any reason, you may request a full refund within 30 days of your purchase.

2. Free Trial Period

Every paid subscription begins with a 7-day free trial. During this period:

  • You have full access to all plan features
  • No credit card is required to start
  • You can cancel anytime without charge
  • If you don't subscribe after the trial, your account automatically converts to the Free plan

3. Refund Policy

3.1 30-Day Refund Guarantee

All FlowCast subscriptions come with a 30-day money-back guarantee. You may request a full refund within 30 days of your purchase for any reason.

3.2 How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Email us at billing@flowcast.space
  • Include your account email and purchase date
  • Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days

3.3 Refund Method

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. If the original payment method is unavailable, we will contact you to arrange an alternative.

4. Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

  • Through your Account Settings on the dashboard
  • By emailing billing@flowcast.space

Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You will continue to have access until then.

5. Processing Time

  • Refund requests: Processed within 5-10 business days
  • Refund method: Issued to the original payment method
  • Currency: Refunds are issued in the original currency of the transaction

6. Paddle Purchases

Payments processed through Paddle are subject to Paddle's Checkout Buyer Terms. For Paddle orders, you may also request refunds directly through paddle.net.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The policy in effect at the time of your purchase applies to your transaction.

10. Contact Us

For refund requests or billing questions:

  • Billing Email: billing@flowcast.space
  • Subject Line: "Refund Request - [Your Account Email]"
  • Response Time: Within 48 hours
We value your business and want you to be happy. If you have any concerns about our service, please reach out. We're here to help and will do our best to find a solution that works for you.
