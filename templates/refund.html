Refund Policy

Last Updated: February 20, 2026

1. Our Commitment

We want you to be completely satisfied with FlowCast. If you're not happy with our service, we'll make it right. This Refund Policy outlines when and how you can request a refund.

Good news: All paid plans include a 7-day free trial, so you can test the full service before committing to a subscription.

2. Free Trial Period

Every paid subscription begins with a 7-day free trial. During this period:

  • You have full access to all plan features
  • No credit card is required to start
  • You can cancel anytime without charge
  • If you don't subscribe after the trial, your account automatically converts to the Free plan

We encourage you to fully test the service during this trial to ensure it meets your needs.

3. Refund Eligibility

3.1 Full Refunds Available

You are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of your first paid subscription if:

  • You experienced technical issues that prevented you from using the service
  • The service did not materially match the description or your reasonable expectations
  • You were accidentally charged due to a billing error

3.2 Partial Refunds

In certain circumstances, we may offer partial refunds:

  • Service outages lasting more than 24 hours in a billing period
  • Significant feature removal or degradation
  • Duplicate charges (refund of the duplicate amount)

3.3 Non-Refundable Situations

We generally cannot provide refunds for:
  • Change of mind after the 14-day refund period
  • Failure to cancel before the next billing cycle
  • Not using the service during your subscription period
  • Violation of our Terms of Service
  • Downgrades (new pricing takes effect next cycle)
  • Partial month usage after the refund period

4. How to Request a Refund

We've made the refund process simple and straightforward:

1

Contact Us

Email us at billing@flowcast.space with the subject line "Refund Request - [Your Account Email]"

2

Provide Details

Include your account email, the date of purchase, and the reason for your refund request. The more details you provide, the faster we can process your request.

3

Review Process

We review all refund requests within 48 hours (usually much faster). We may contact you for additional information if needed.

4

Refund Issued

If approved, refunds are processed within 5-10 business days to your original payment method. You'll receive a confirmation email.

5. Cancellation vs. Refund

It's important to understand the difference:

Cancellation: Stops future billing. You keep access until the end of your current billing period. No refund is issued for the current period.

Refund: Returns money for a payment already made. May result in immediate loss of access depending on the circumstances.

5.1 How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription anytime:

  • Through your Account Settings on the dashboard
  • By emailing billing@flowcast.space

Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period. You'll continue to have access until then.

6. Processing Time and Methods

6.1 Timeline

  • Refund request review: Within 48 hours
  • Refund processing: 5-10 business days after approval
  • Funds appear in your account: Depends on your bank (typically 3-5 additional business days)

6.2 Refund Method

All refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase. If that method is no longer available (e.g., expired credit card), we will contact you to arrange an alternative.

6.3 Currency

Refunds are issued in US Dollars ($), the original currency of the transaction. The amount you receive may vary slightly due to exchange rate fluctuations if your bank account is in a different currency.

7. Special Circumstances

7.1 Annual Billing

If you pre-paid for an annual subscription, refunds are calculated based on the monthly equivalent rate for the time used, minus any applicable discounts.

7.2 Add-ons and Extras

Refunds for add-ons (additional team members, extra posts, etc.) follow the same 14-day policy and are evaluated on a case-by-case basis.

7.3 Fraud Prevention

To protect against fraud, we may require additional verification before processing refunds. This may include confirming your identity or providing proof of the issue.

8. Disputes and Chargebacks

We encourage you to contact us first if you have a billing issue. We're usually able to resolve problems quickly and amicably.

If you initiate a chargeback through your bank or credit card company:

  • Your account may be suspended pending investigation
  • We will provide evidence to the payment processor
  • Fraudulent chargebacks may result in account termination
  • We reserve the right to contest invalid chargebacks

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes are effective immediately upon posting. The policy in effect at the time of your purchase applies to your transaction.

10. Contact Us

For refund requests or billing questions:

  • Billing Email: billing@flowcast.space
  • Subject Line: "Refund Request - [Your Account Email]"
  • Response Time: Within 48 hours
We value your business and want you to be happy. If you have any concerns about our service, please reach out. We're here to help and will do our best to find a solution that works for you.
