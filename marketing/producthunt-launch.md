## First Comment Strategy

### The Launch Story

```
Hey Product Hunt! 👋

I'm Daz, solo founder of FlowCast. Super excited to share this with you all.

**Why I built this:**

I've been a content creator for 2 years. Fitness niche. 50K followers across platforms.

And I was drowning in the "planning phase."

Every Sunday: 3 hours scrolling TikTok, checking competitors, rewriting hooks.
For maybe 2-3 decent ideas.

The math broke me:
3 hours × 4 weeks = 12 hours/month planning
For ~10 videos = 1.2 hours per video just to PLAN

That's when I realized: I don't have an editing problem. I have an IDEAS problem.

**What FlowCast does:**

It generates personalized content ideas in 30 seconds.

Not generic "10 video ideas for fitness creators" blog posts.

Actual ideas calibrated to:
• Your specific niche
• Your content tone
• Your target audience
• Current trends in your space

Plus platform-native versions (TikTok ≠ Reels ≠ Shorts) and hook variations.

**What makes it different:**

• Pre-production focus (vs editing tools like OpusClip)
• Personalization engine (learns from your feedback)
• Platform-native outputs (not copy-paste)
• Affordable pricing ($12/mo vs $50+ for competitors)

**The ask:**

If you're a creator (or know one), try the free trial. 7 days, full features, no credit card.

I'd love your feedback—reply here or email me directly at daz@flowcast.space.

Also happy to answer any questions! Drop them below 👇

---

P.S. If you upvote and comment, I'll personally generate 5 custom content ideas for your niche. Just reply with your niche and platform!
```
