# FlowCast vs Planable vs Notion - Competitive Analysis
## February 2026

---

## Executive Summary

**FlowCast** competes in the content planning/scheduling space but with different positioning:
- **Planable** = Premium team collaboration + approvals
- **Notion** = DIY content calendar (manual, no auto-posting)
- **FlowCast** = Affordable AI-powered scheduling for creators

---

### Planable Strengths (What They Do Better)

1. **Visual Post Previews**
   - Shows exactly how posts will look on each platform
   - Side-by-side comparison
   - FlowCast: Basic text-only currently

2. **Approval Workflows**
   - Multi-level approvals (manager → client)
   - Comment and annotate on posts
   - Approval history/audit trail
   - FlowCast: No approval system (yet)

3. **Team Collaboration**
   - Built for agencies with multiple clients
   - Client-specific workspaces
   - Role-based permissions
   - FlowCast: Simple team features (Pro tier)

4. **Visual Calendar**
   - Rich drag-and-drop interface
   - Post previews in calendar
   - Color-coded labels
   - FlowCast: Functional but simpler

5. **Mobile App**
   - iOS and Android apps
   - Approve posts on-the-go
   - FlowCast: Web-only currently

---

### Notion Strengths (What They Do Better)

1. **Flexibility**
   - Fully customizable databases
   - Build any workflow you want
   - Templates for everything
   - FlowCast: Opinionated workflow (faster setup)

2. **All-in-One Workspace**
   - Docs, wikis, tasks, calendar in one
   - Connect content to strategy docs
   - FlowCast: Focused only on social media

3. **Knowledge Management**
   - Content library and assets
   - SOPs and brand guidelines
   - FlowCast: No knowledge base features

4. **Price (for general use)**
   - $8/month for unlimited personal use
   - Not just for social media
   - FlowCast: Social media specific

---

### FlowCast Strengths (Where We Win)

1. **AI Content Generation**
   - Built-in AI ideas (Gemini, DeepSeek, GPT-4)
   - 5-25 ideas per day (free to starter)
   - Unlimited for paid tiers
   - Planable: ❌ No AI features
   - Notion: ⚠️ AI add-on ($10/month extra)

2. **Price**
   - €9/month vs Planable $39/month
   - 78% cheaper than Planable
   - Best value for solo creators

3. **YouTube Integration**
   - OAuth connection to YouTube
   - Video scheduling
   - Channel analytics
   - Planable: ❌ Weak YouTube support
   - Notion: ❌ No auto-posting

4. **Speed to Value**
   - 2-minute setup
   - AI generates ideas immediately
   - Start scheduling today
   - Notion: Requires building your own system

5. **Creator-Focused**
   - Built for individual creators, not agencies
   - No complex approval workflows
   - Simple, fast, affordable

6. **EU-Friendly**
   - Paddle handles VAT
   - GDPR compliant
   - EU-based alternatives available (Mistral)
