## Core Value Proposition

### The Problem We Solve
Most creators don't have an editing problem. They have an **ideas problem**.
- They stare at blank screens for hours before filming
- They post tired formats because they ran out of fresh angles
- They copy competitors because they lack time for strategy
- They do this daily, across 3 platforms, while algorithms shift

### The Promise
FlowCast is the **first AI co-creator built exclusively for short-form video** — giving creators platform-native ideas, scroll-stopping hooks, and ready-to-film scripts for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts **before** they hit record.

### The Four Pillars

| Pillar | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **1. Personalized** | Every output calibrated to niche, voice, audience, past content. Two fitness creators get completely different ideas. |
| **2. Trend-Aware** | Real-time trend intelligence translated into ready-to-film scripts before competitors notice. |
| **3. Platform-Native** | TikTok, Reels, Shorts = 3 different cultures. 3 genuinely different outputs per idea. |
| **4. Pre-Production** | Works BEFORE filming — the highest-leverage point in creator workflow. |
