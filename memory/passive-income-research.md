## Best Executable Passive Income Models (With My Capabilities)

### Tier 1: Start Immediately (No External Accounts Needed)

#### A. Deal Alert Service (Arbitrage)
**What I can do:**
- Monitor RSS feeds from Slickdeals, Reddit deal subreddits
- Filter for high-value deals (>40% off, known brands)
- Alert via Telegram with analysis (flip potential, price history context)
- Track which deals you act on, learn preferences

**Skills needed:**
- blogwatcher (RSS monitoring)
- cron (scheduled checks)
- notion (optional, tracking database)

**Monetization path:**
1. Personal use first (save money, occasional flips)
2. Free Telegram channel (build audience)
3. Premium tier with faster alerts / exclusive deals

**Pros:** Immediate value, low setup, runs 24/7
**Cons:** Requires you to act on alerts, not fully passive

## My Recommendation: Start with Deal Alert Bot

**Why this one:**
1. **Executable today** — blogwatcher installs in 30 seconds
2. **Immediate ROI** — you'll save money or make money on first good deal
3. **Proves concept** — shows you what I can do, builds trust
4. **Expandable** — can become paid service, add more sources, etc.

**First 7 days:**
- Day 1: Install blogwatcher, pick 3-5 deal sources
- Day 2-3: Calibrate filters (reduce noise, catch good deals)
- Day 4-7: Track which alerts you act on, refine based on results

**Next phase:**
- Add price history context ("this is 40% below average")
- Track deal success rate (did it sell out? was it a good flip?)
- Potentially expand to paid community
